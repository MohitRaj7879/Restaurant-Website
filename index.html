About Us

Zero Point Restaurant is established in the winter season of 2023. This is the dream of a mother which is fulfilled by her son.

At Zero Point, we believe that every dish tells a story. From the sizzling pans to the delicate plating, our menu is a journey through the heart of Mohit and his mother's culinary heritage. Each recipe is a nod to the comforting embrace of home-cooked meals and the joyous moments shared around the family table. I really want to thank my business partner Dolly Mishra, who is an important pillar of this franchise.Thank you for being a part of our story.

Mohit Raj
Zero Point Restaurant And Bar
